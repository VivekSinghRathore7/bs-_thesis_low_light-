RetinexRestormer — 7-Channel Input Construction

I_low (3ch) ‖ R_low (3ch) ‖ I_tv (1ch) → Restormer U-Net → Enhanced (3ch)

I_low — Low-Light Image (3ch)

The original low-light input image in BGR format. Passed directly as the first 3 channels of the 7-channel input — provides raw color and brightness information.

R_low — Reflectance (3ch)

Computed via Retinex theory: R = I_low / (I_smooth + ε). The illumination-invariant surface color. Bilateral-filtered (d=9, σ_c=25, σ_s=10) for denoising. Channels 4–6 of the input.

I_tv — Illumination Map (1ch)

Estimated via max(B,G,R) per pixel, then bilateral-smoothed (d=15, σ_c=75, σ_s=75). Captures the scene lighting structure. Channel 7 of the input.

Retinex Theory: S = R × I

A scene S is the product of Reflectance R (intrinsic surface color) and Illumination I (lighting). By decomposing the low-light image, the model gets explicit structural priors about lighting and color, enabling better enhancement.
